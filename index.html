We spend five sevenths of our lives waging in fluorescent cages. To live diagonal is to open your cage and walk out.

Live Diagonal is a community whose work distills and radiates life instead of adding to its noise. We view technology as a means to more intentional living and hold communal success above all as the true goal of life.

Simplicity

Staying alive is pretty easy. Avoid lions. Eat food. Stay warm. It becomes stressful as you let this list grow. Change the oil. Go Christmas shopping. Pay the credit cards. The Live Diagonal Marketplace exists to help you navigate these difficult lifestyle decisions.

Technology

We are available for technical consultation on products aligned with our mission.

We build software to help our community achieve its goals. Our Queue Ball app is free for all ambitious, minimalist creatives looking to do some Spring cleaning on their lives.

Community

You can think of our community as something between a music label and the mob. We are a semi-decentralized network of self-motivated innovators working together where natural to accomplish disparate goals.

Where others invest in companies, we invest in people. We are building a network of talented individials and we share equally in the value that we produce.
